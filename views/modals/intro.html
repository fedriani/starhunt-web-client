  • Move around the sky by clicking and dragging the Field of View.
  • Zoom in/out by scrolling the mouse wheel, pressing -/+ or Page Up/Page Down.
  • Drag with center mouse button or hold Ctrl while dragging to rotate and tilt view.
  • Click an object’s thumbnail to pan to the object. Double-clicking the object’s thumbnail jumps you to the object.
  • Page through multiple thumbnail panes with the scroll wheel
  • Right-click an object to display the contextual Finder Scope for more information.
  • Menu Tabs have two parts: click the tab’s top to open a pane, click the tab’s bottom to open submenus with additional functionality.
